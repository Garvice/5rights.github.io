THE RIGHT BEERS

We offer a broad range of thoughtfully and carefully crafted beers that will appeal to many different palates. All of us here at 5 Rights take pride in finding the Right Beer choice to fit your taste and experience.

OTC (OVER-THE-COUNTER) BEERS

These are the full range of staple remedy beers that we offer at 5 Rights, from low gravity session beers to our house OTC IPA. Also included are our Gateway series offerings which were designed as a full-flavored bridge for those who are just beginning to explore the world of craft beer. Heard of gateway drugs? We have Gateway beers. And once you start there’s no going back.

RX (Prescription) Beers

When Over-the-Counter remedies just aren’t enough. We offer higher gravity and seasonal beers for when you need a little extra attention to your craft beer cravings. This includes our regular series of Imperial IPA’s, seasonal Saisons and other seasonally inspired creations.

Nurse's Order Beers

When it comes to craft beer we believe it is totally acceptable to be high maintenance. Some things in life need extra special care, like the type of extra care and attention that we put into our Nurse’s Orders selections. These are our special release beers including our yearly aged Winter saison, Russian Imperial Stour, and other high gravity creations. Nurses know best.

WHY '5 Rights'?

While Kristi was training how not to kill people in nursing school she was taught the “5 Rights of Medication Administration” which are: Right Medication; Right Dose; Right Time; Right Route and Right Patient. Failing to check these “Five Rights” could lead to harming your patient, or even worse killing your patient. It is a tool used by nurses across the world to ensure the safe and thoughtful practice of medicine.


When we named our brewery Kristi and R.J. thought this concept was a natural fit to express our passion for doing what’s right for our customers. We at 5 Rights practice thoughtful brewing and care deeply about the product we share with our family and friends. Excellence in standard and practice ensure that the beer we make is worthy of our name. To quote Florence Nightingale: “I attribute my success to this: I never gave or took any excuse.
